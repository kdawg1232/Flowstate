Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 31, 2025

FlowState ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the FlowState mobile application.

1. Data Collection

FlowState is designed as a "local-first" application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, health data, or activity history to external servers. All progress, reps, and scores are stored strictly on your device's local storage.

2. Third-Party Services

Our app does not use any third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising SDKs. We do not share any data with third parties.

3. Account Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, deleting the app or using the "Delete Account & Data" feature within the app settings will permanently erase all your information. We do not maintain any backups of your data.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@flowstate-app.com.
